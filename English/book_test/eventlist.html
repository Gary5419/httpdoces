Toky Bay Cruise

Enjoy the Yokosuka in two cruise! Yokosuka naval port Cruise & Sashima expedition cruise (Kannonzaki Keikyu Hotel lunch buffet)

Only cruising tour in Japan where you can see up close the ship of the United States Navy and the Maritime Self-Defense Force! Maritime Self-Defense Force of submarines and destroyers, lots such as the US Navy's Aegis. Cruising while close to the views of the ships of the strong force is the charm of Yokosuka unique. （45分のクルージング）

Holding period From Jun. 30 2015 To Sep. 21. 2015
Destination Kanagawa - Yokosuka
Price Min.9,980 yen（Bus fare + boarding fee + meals + tour operator fee）
